Statement of Faith

As a Christ-centered institution, we stand on the following unshakeable truths:

  • 1. The Scriptures

    We believe the Bible to be the inspired, the only infallible, authoritative Word of God.

  • 2. The Godhead

    We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

  • 3. The Deity of Christ

    We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, and in His vicarious and atoning death.

  • 4. Salvation

    We believe that for the salvation of lost and sinful man, regeneration by the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential.

  • 5. The Great Commission

    We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ and the duty to go into all the world and preach the Gospel.
