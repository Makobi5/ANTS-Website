About ANTS

ANTS is a Tertiary Institution registered by NCHE in Uganda. ANTS was founded in January, 1993 as a joint project of East Africa (Uganda), Korea and American Christians. Its purpose is to train international Christians as pastors, church leaders, and missionaries for the growth and maturity of all churches in Africa and the world. Currently students from (Uganda, Kenya, South Sudan, DR Congo, Rwanda, Burundi, Tanzania, Ethiopia, etc) are studying at this institute.

Our Mission

The mission of ANTS is to prepare men and women through Biblical teachings to serve the church and the society by making disciples of all nations.

Our Vision

The Vision of ANTS, is to be a Center of Spiritual and Academic excellence in training Christian leaders in East Africa and beyond.

CORE VALUES

  1. Christ-Centered
  2. Reformed Biblical Theology
  3. Intimate Relationship With God
  4. Self Reliance and Responsibility
  5. Excellence in Academics and Ministry
  6. Kingdom of God
  7. Global Leadership in African Context
  8. Mission Driven
