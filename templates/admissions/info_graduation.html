Graduation Requirements

Graduation is the culmination of your academic journey at ANTS. Below are the procedures and requirements to qualify.

Semesters & Credits

Students must finish semesters and get credits suggested as below. If students do not earn the suggested credits, their graduation will be delayed.

  • Certificate Program 80 Credits
  • Diploma Program 72 Credits

Completed in four (4) semesters.

Bible Knowledge Test

Students have to pass the 'Bible Knowledge Test'.

Requirement:

Pass 3 out of 4 times during 4 semesters.

Procedures of Graduation

  1. The Committee shall have a meeting to assess all candidates for graduation after the end of their final semester.
  2. Transcripts shall be prepared by the registrar for review.
  3. The Committee shall determine the day of graduation considering the academic schedule.
  4. The result of assessment and the date of graduation ceremony will be informed to all candidates.
