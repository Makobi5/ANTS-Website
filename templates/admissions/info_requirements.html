Qualification of Admission

Admission into the ANTS program is for people who meet the following conditions based on National Council for Higher Education (NCHE) standards.

1. Ordinary Certificate (2 Years)

The applicant for the 2-year 'Ordinary Certificate' must have completed:

  • Senior Four (S4, 'O' Level) with at least 3 passes in UCE (Uganda Certificate of Education) obtained at the same sitting.
2. Ordinary Diploma (2 Years)

The applicant for the 2-year 'Ordinary Diploma' must have completed:

  • Senior Four (S4, 'O' Level) with minimum 5 passes in UCE.
  • Senior Six (S6, 'A' Level) with minimum 1 principal pass and 2 subsidiary passes in UACE obtained at the same sitting.
  • OR a Certificate Programme in ANTS.
Note on Special Cases

Students admitted without the minimum entry requirements as per NCHE can only receive an award of Certificate of Attendance in Biblical Studies. The applicant must be active in some form of local church or Christian ministry.
