Why Study at All Nations Theological College & Seminary

ANTS is dedicated to equipping servant leaders for the Great Commission. Here is why you should choose us for your theological journey.

Fully Registered & Accredited

We are recognized by the National Council for Higher Education (NCHE). This ensures that your academic papers from ANTS are recognized worldwide as valid. When you enroll with ANTS, you are joining an institution that meets national and international standards.

Christ-Centered Curriculum

Our curriculum is rooted in the unshakeable truth of Scripture. We emphasize practical ministry skills alongside academic rigor. Whether you are called to pastoral ministry, missions, or counseling, our courses are designed to deepen your faith and sharpen your mind.

Affordable Education & Scholarships

We believe theological education should be accessible. ANTS offers highly competitive tuition rates. Additionally, we offer bursaries and work-study programs to qualifying students who demonstrate financial need and academic potential.

Holistic Student Development

ANTS is not just about books; it is about life transformation. Our serene campus environment in Kisubi allows for spiritual reflection. We emphasize community life, daily chapel services, and mentorship from experienced faculty.

Resourceful Library

Our library is stocked with a wide selection of theological books, commentaries, and online journals to support your research. We provide a quiet environment conducive to deep study and reflection.
