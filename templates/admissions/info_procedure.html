Procedure of Admission

1
Interview Schedule

The Committee of Admission and Graduation shall announce the interview schedule at the end of each semester.

2
Document Collection

The Committee shall collect the documents required for admission from students one (1) day prior to the interview through the office of the College.

3
Entrance Tests

The Applicants must take tests regarding their Bible Knowledge and English Proficiency before their interview with the Committee.

4
Assessment & Decision

Assessments shall take place immediately after the end of interview, followed by the official announcement of decision written/attached on the board of the College.

Documents Required

  • Completed Application form.
  • Testimony of Christian conversion.
  • Two passport-sized photographs.
  • Recommendation letters from Senior Pastor.
  • Original and photocopies of academic certificates/transcripts.
  • Valid National ID or Passport.
  • Medical History Form.
