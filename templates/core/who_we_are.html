Who We Are

All Nations Theological College and Seminary (ANTS) is an interdenominational institution committed to training men and women for the Gospel ministry.

Our History

Founded in 2004, ANTS began with a vision to equip leaders in Uganda who lacked formal theological training. From a humble beginning with just 12 students, the college has grown into a premier institution attracting students from across East Africa.

Our Philosophy

We believe in "Head, Heart, and Hands" training. It is not enough to know the Bible intellectually; one must be transformed spiritually and equipped practically for service.
